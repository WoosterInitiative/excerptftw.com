{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}} **Copy Paste Helper** is a website dedicated to collecting credible sources with useful excerpts to be copied into social media posts and comments. For those times that you *know* that someone has written a great article or paper about this topic, but you don't have the time or energy to find and format it. Each source and excerpt has a *Copy to Clipboard* button to make it super simple to copy and paste the comment, with links, to your chosen social media platform. See [How To]({{< relref "/pages/how-to" >}}) for how to use it for each platform. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="light" >}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-lightbulb" title="Simple To Use" %}} **Copy Paste Helper** is designed to be as easy as possible for creating comments on as many social media platforms as we can. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fab fa-github" title="Contributions welcome!" url="https://github.com/WoosterInitiative/excerptftw.com" %}} We do a [Pull Request](https://github.com/WoosterInitiative/excerptftw.com/pulls) contributions workflow on **GitHub**. New contributors are always welcome! If you're not comfortable with **GitHub**, use the **Submit** button on the right-hand side of every topic page. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-question" title="Why Does This Exist?" url="/about" %}} This project was founded out of a personal desire to have good content at our fingertips. Social media has made is so easy to share content that has been served to us. This leads to a lot of "I know I could find something to respond to this with," but if we're being honest, who's going to go find that data and present it in a compelling way. This was our answer. Let us know what you think! {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}}
